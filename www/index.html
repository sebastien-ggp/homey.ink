What is this?

Homey.ink is a wall-mounted dashboard for Homey, made for e-readers with e-ink display technology. Simply put, it's a tailor-made webpage on the e-reader's web browser.

Due to the e-ink technology, the power consumption of the dashboard is extremely low.
